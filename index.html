About Me

Hello, my name is Jaden and I am currently an incoming Masters student at UCLA studying Aerospace Engineering. I have recently graduated from the University of California, Berkeley with a B.S. in Aerospace Engineering as a part of the major's inaugural class. I am interested in all things propulsion and aerodynamics, a passion I have cultivated throughout my undergraduate degree. I believe that the exploration and inhabitation of space is essential for humanity's future, and I am determined to push us towards this reality.

Through my experiences in classes, student teams, research, internships, and personal projects, I have developed a versatile skill set in the design, simulation, manufacturing, and testing of complex systems. From conducting aerodynamic optimization research at NASA Ames Research Center to supporting thruster hot-fire testing at Vast, to designing multiple iterations of rocket valves on my student team I have built a strong foundation in aerodynamics and propulsion systems.

Please feel free to contact me at jaden.dew1@gmail.com or via LinkedIn!
